Islands of Adventure

     This park contains seven different islands, each with unique theming to a different Universal Studio’s movie.

     This Port of Entry is the main entry to the park, and following the hub and spokes concept, contains no attractions. Within the section are several shops and food vendors, as well as a lighthouse that sends out a bright beam of light to guide visitors to and from the entrance at night.

     Marvel Super Hero Island is a section focuses around Marvel superheroes, including Spiderman, Superman, Captain America, and The Hulk. It contains primarily thrilling rides and the buildings are shaped, as they would be in comic books, with sharp exaggerated lines and angles.

     The Toon Lagoon is primarily water based and appeals to both children and adults. Various attractions allow visitors to beat the summer heat and even get other guests more wet.

     The jurassic park area is based around the movie Jurassic park and contains some thrill rides as well as discovery and education based exhibits. There are a variety of vendors that sell dinosaur related toys and foods.

     The Wizarding World of Harry Potter is the newest addition to the Islands, this section is geared around the imaginary world of Harry Potter in J. K. Rowling’s books. There are several attractions and all sorts of interesting places to try different foods and drinks.

     The Lost Continent section of the park is based entirely on myths. It is home to many shows centering around characters such as Poseidon. There are also places to eat and shop.

     Seuss is a unique park that is geared towards small children. It’s based on the artwork of Dr. Seuss and contains Seuss themed attractions such as The Green Eggs and Ham café and a Dr. Seuss train ride.
