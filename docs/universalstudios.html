Universal Studios

     Universal Studios Florida focuses around the entertainment industry. It has six unique sections all focused around a lagoon.

     Production Central serves as the opening section of the park and features motion picture soundstages that each contains either a real studio or a unique park attraction. There are also several merchants in the area that sell a variety of foods, drinks, and souvenirs.

     This part of the park is aptly named; the architecture is modeled to resemble that of past and present New York City. There are vendors and few park attractions, including the Delancey Street Preview Center, which allows the studio to collect feedback from guests.

     The San Francisco section of the park includes attractions such as Jaws and a ride that allows the guest to put him or herself into the place of an action star. There are vendors who sell food throughout the section.

     World Expo combines architecture from the 1960s and architecture of today. Theming consists of various flags from around the world. This section currently hosts both Simpsons and a Men in Black themed attractions.

     Woody Woodpecker's Kidzone is the park mascot, this section hosts kid friendly attractions including an ET themed show and a rollercoaster. Similarly themed shops are spread throughout.

     The Hollywood section of the park contains Hollywood themed attractions and monuments.
